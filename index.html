About Dawson Gant

Dawson Gant got into business early — starting in real estate at 18, then moving into managed services, sales operations, and e-commerce. Over the years he's built and operated businesses across several industries.

Now based in Miami, Dawson Gant works across three areas: growth operations, real estate, and telehealth. He takes a hands-on approach — building teams and systems rather than just investing from the sidelines.
